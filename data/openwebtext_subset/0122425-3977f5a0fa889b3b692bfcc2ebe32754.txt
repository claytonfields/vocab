Do you love your hybrid, fear for the plight of the polar bears, dying to put solar panels on your roof? Then you might be a member of the eco-elite. Not that there's anything wrong with you, says Van Jones , but for a green economy to be truly effective, it needs to be more inclusive.This is the thrust of The Green Collar Economy , Van Jones' new book. The very quotable Jones is up there among the world's brightest , seeking a green economy "that is strong enough to pull millions of people out of poverty."
